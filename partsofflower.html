here are the basics:

PISTEL:

the pistel is the reproductive part of the flower. It contains the stigma, style, and ovary. Pollen is transfered through the stigma and makes it way down the flower. Once the pollen makes it to the ovary fertilization can begin.

SEPAL

the leafy outer part of the flower that covers the bud

PETAL

the often colorful part right inside of the sepal

STAMEN

the part that produces pollen. usually located inside and at the base of the flower
